Frequently Asked Questions

We collect user data such as wallet addresses and transaction details to process transactions and enhance user experience. Your data is stored securely and is protected from unauthorized access. We do not share your data with third parties except for essential service providers.

Participants must meet eligibility criteria, including age restrictions and compliance with local laws. Users are responsible for providing accurate information and adhering to our rules. We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time.

You can cancel your ticket before the draw date and receive a partial refund. A 10% cancellation fee will be deducted, and the refund amount will be transferred back to your wallet. No refunds are available after the draw is executed.

We implement robust security measures, including smart contract audits and secure data storage, to protect your transactions and personal information. Users are encouraged to protect their wallet credentials and be vigilant against phishing attempts.

Each ticket costs 1 ETH, and there is a limit of 5 tickets per user for each draw. When you purchase a ticket, you receive a unique ticket number between 0000 and 9999 associated with your wallet address.

The lottery draw occurs after a set interval, usually every few minutes, using a secure random number generator. The draw will be automatically executed if there are at least 3 participants in the draw.

The prize pool consists of the total ETH collected, minus a 5% developer fee. The remaining amount is distributed with 50% going to the first prize, 30% to the second, and 20% to the third prize. Prizes are transferred directly to the winner's wallet addresses after the draw.

If there are no winners for a specific prize tier, the prize will be rolled over to the next draw, increasing the potential prize for that tier in the future draw.
